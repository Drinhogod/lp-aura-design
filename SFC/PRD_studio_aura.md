# Project Brief & PRD: Studio Aura Landing Page

**Client:** Marina Vasconcelos (Studio Aura)  
**Project:** High-End Architecture & Interior Design Landing Page  
**Status:** Design Phase (V1 Completed)

---

## 1. Project Overview
The objective is to create a sophisticated, high-fidelity landing page for **Studio Aura**, a boutique interior design firm based in São Paulo. The page must function as a digital brand experience, conveying "discreet luxury" and "art gallery" aesthetics to attract high-net-worth clients.

## 2. Target Audience
- High-end residential clients in São Paulo.
- Real estate developers looking for luxury staging/interiors.
- Luxury lifestyle enthusiasts who value exclusivity and detail.

## 3. Core Visual Identity (Design System)
The design follows the **Studio Aura** design system (ID: {{DATA:DESIGN_SYSTEM:DESIGN_SYSTEM_1}}):
- **Palette:** Obsidian black (#0F0F0F), charcoal (#131313), and brushed gold accents (#D4AF37).
- **Typography:** Bodoni Moda (Serif) for high-contrast headlines; minimalist Sans-serif for body copy.
- **Mood:** Sophisticated, dark, editorial, boutique hotel vibe.

## 4. Key Sections & Requirements
- **Hero Section:** Full-screen immersive experience using `interior-dark-video.mp4`. Minimalist typography with a strong value proposition.
- **Process Section:** "A Jornada" (The Journey). Three-step narrative (Conception, Development, Execution) integrating `video-camadas.mp4`.
- **Portfolio:** "Projetos Selecionados". A curated gallery of 7 projects using an asymmetrical grid layout.
- **About Us:** Profile of founder Marina Vasconcelos, highlighting her visionary approach and experience.
- **Social Proof:** "Experiências" section featuring testimonials from high-profile clients.
- **Conversion:** Minimalist contact form and a direct "WhatsApp Direct" call-to-action.
- **Footer:** Links to social media (Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest) and legal info.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Framework:** Responsive HTML/CSS.
- **Device Support:** Optimized for Desktop (primary focus) and Mobile.
- **Assets:** 
    - Video: `interior-dark-video.mp4` (Hero), `video-camadas.mp4` (Process).
    - Images: 7 curated interior project photos.
